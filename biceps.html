BICEPS

On the front of the upper arms is a prominent muscle that crosses both your shoulder and elbow joints, is used to bend your elbows, turn your palms outwards and inwards, assist the shoulder in moving your arms forward and upwards, and assist in stabilising the shoulder. These muscles are commonly referred to as just your ‘biceps’.
